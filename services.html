Service Center

Detailed services for every stage of vehicle care.

Diagnostics Service

We run a full electronic and mechanical diagnostic to quickly find the root cause of engine lights, power loss, electrical faults, and sensor errors.

  • Battery, alternator, and starter health check
  • Sensor and wiring verification
  • Road-test validation and repair priority plan

General Maintenance

Preventive maintenance packages are designed around your mileage and driving conditions to reduce breakdown risk and improve fuel economy.

  • Engine oil and filter replacement
  • Cooling and brake fluid inspection/top-up

  • Belts, hoses, and tire condition checks

Brake & Suspension

We inspect and service key safety systems to improve stopping distance, steering response, and ride stability.

  • Wheel alignment recommendations
  • Post-service road safety check

  • Interior steam cleaning and fabric treatment
  • Paint correction and swirl removal
